Strongest Predictors

Based on statistical research and meta-analyses

1. Before Interaction (Publicly Observable)

Factors visible without direct interaction.

1

High Sociosexuality

Unrestricted openness to casual sex. Visible through public social behavior, openly casual dating lifestyle, or stated attitudes.

2

Relationship Instability

Public history of short, unstable, or frequently changing relationships.

3

Impulsivity / Sensation-Seeking

Lifestyle characterized by constant parties, risky choices, and extreme novelty chasing.

4

Alcohol & Party Culture

Heavy binge-drinking and strong identification with nightlife/clubbing culture.

5

Narcissistic Self-Presentation

Excessive self-promotional imagery and attention-seeking behavior online.

2. After Interaction (Before Commitment)

Factors visible after talking/dating but before exclusivity.

1

Unrestricted Sociosexual Attitudes

Directly expressed views like "I don't believe in monogamy" or "I get bored fast".

2

History of Infidelity

Admission of cheating in past relationships. The strongest behavioral predictor.

3

Low Past Relationship Satisfaction

Chronically feeling "unfulfilled", "bored", or "emotionally disconnected" in prior relationships.

4

Insecure Attachment Style

Avoidant (fear of closeness) or Anxious (clingy/inconsistent) attachment patterns.

5

High Perceived Options

Belief in having many romantic options and viewing relationships as temporary.
